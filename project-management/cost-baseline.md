## Cost Notes

**Machinery (C-01 — largest cost driver)**  
Manufacturing new industrial bakery equipment in-house at INGEDAV S.R.L. reduced costs
compared to importing equivalent machinery. The kneader, laminator, and mixers were built
to sanitary regulations with higher capacity and energy efficiency than the replaced equipment.

**Electrical modernization (C-02)**  
Motor replacement was the primary lever for achieving the -27% steady-state energy reduction
documented in the field report. Replacing motors older than 5 years on all production
equipment eliminated the main sources of excessive kWh consumption and power losses.

**EMS (C-03)**  
The Energy Management System enables ongoing monitoring of electricity consumption across
the plant. It is both a control tool and a data source for future optimization, leaving the
client with the infrastructure to sustain and extend the efficiency gains beyond the project.

**Budget adherence**  
The project was executed within the approved USD 85,000 budget. The ~5% contingency
reserve within C-06 absorbed minor unforeseen civil works discovered during the Phase 1
site survey.
