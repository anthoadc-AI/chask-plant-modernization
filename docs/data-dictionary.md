**File**: `data/raw/dataset_panificadora.csv`

| `consumo_kwh` | float | kWh | Total electrical energy consumed by the plant. | EMS / electricity bill |

| `ventas_usd` | float | USD | Total monthly revenue from product sales. | Sales records |
| `costos_usd` | float | USD | Total monthly operating costs (energy + labor + materials). | Cost records |
| `tiempo_inactividad_horas` | float | hours | Total production downtime (planned + unplanned) per month. | Operations log |

## Synthetic Data Disclosure

> **This dataset is entirely synthetic and must not be used as real measurements.**

### What is synthetic

All rows in `daily_synthetic.csv` are computer-generated. No daily operational data was
collected during the project; only monthly aggregates were recorded.

### How it was generated

The generator (`src/chask/datagen/synthetic.py`) distributes each monthly aggregate across
the calendar days of that month using:

1. **Weekday seasonality**: relative production weights per day of week (higher on
   Friday/Saturday, lower on Sunday) reflecting typical bakery operations.
2. **Multiplicative lognormal noise** (σ = 0.15) for day-to-day variability.
3. **Rescaling**: each daily weight vector is rescaled so the sum exactly matches the
   monthly total.
4. **Failure events**: `fallas_maquina` and `mantenimiento` are distributed as binary
   daily events (0 or 1) sampled without replacement, so the monthly count is preserved
   exactly.
5. **Consumption on failure days**: failure days receive a 30% upward weight for
   `consumo_kwh` before rescaling, reflecting wasted energy during unplanned stops.

### Calibration guarantee

For every calendar month, the sum of daily values equals the corresponding monthly value:

- Continuous variables (`produccion_kg`, `consumo_kwh`, `ventas_usd`, `costos_usd`,
  `tiempo_inactividad_horas`): exact within floating-point precision (rtol ≤ 1e-9).
- Discrete events (`fallas_maquina`, `mantenimiento`): exact integer match.

This is verified by the test suite (`tests/test_synthetic.py::test_calibration_*`).

### Seed

**Fixed seed**: `RANDOM_SEED = 42` (defined in `chask.config`). Two runs with the same
seed produce identical output (verified by `tests/test_synthetic.py::test_reproducibility`).

### Intended use

The synthetic daily dataset is intended for:

- Visualizations requiring sub-monthly granularity (time-series plots, Streamlit dashboard).
- Anomaly detection algorithm development and testing (Z-score, Isolation Forest).
- Demonstration and educational purposes.

It is **not suitable** for: precise financial reporting, regulatory submissions, or any
analysis where the accuracy of individual daily measurements is required.

## Known Data Caveats

### 1. Small post-intervention sample (n = 9)

The post-intervention period covers only 9 months (Sep 2021 – May 2022). Statistical tests
on this period have limited power. Effect size (Cohen's d) should always be reported
alongside p-values to assess practical significance.

### 2. Post period includes commissioning/stabilization phase

The new machinery became fully operational in August 2021. The following months
(Sep 2021 onward) include the commissioning and calibration period, during which:

- Machine failures increased: 7.3 → 10.2 per month (+39.7%).
- Downtime increased slightly: 28.3 → 29.1 h/month (+2.8%).
- Production dipped: 13,881 → 13,207 kg/month (−4.9%).

These are expected transitional effects, not permanent regressions. **Do not interpret
post-period averages alone as the steady-state performance of the new equipment.**

### 3. Distinction between dataset averages and steady-state field measurements

The engineering closure report documents steady-state measurements after full calibration:

- Energy reduction: −27% (55,000 → 40,000 kWh/month).
- Capacity increase: +50%.
- Productivity improvement: +22%.

These figures were measured in controlled conditions and differ from the monthly dataset
averages (which include the stabilization phase). When citing results, always specify
whether you are quoting **monthly dataset averages** or **field-documented steady-state
measurements**.

### 4. No daily raw data

No daily operational data was collected. The daily synthetic dataset is a model-generated
approximation, not a record of actual daily operations.
